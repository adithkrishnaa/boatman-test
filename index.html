    India's Finest Semi Husked Coconuts - Exported Worldwide

    Welcome to Boatman Overseas, your trusted source for premium semi husked coconuts from the heart of Pollachi, Tamil Nadu, India. We specialize in exporting the finest quality coconuts to destinations all around the globe.

    ABOUT US

    At Boatman Overseas, we're passionate about connecting the world with the freshest semi husked coconuts from Pollachi, Tamil Nadu, India. Our mission is to deliver premium quality coconuts directly from farmers to customers worldwide. With a focus on sustainability and customer satisfaction, we strive to uphold Pollachi's agricultural heritage while meeting global demands. Experience the taste of Pollachi with us today.

    OUR PROCESS

    At Boatman Overseas, we take pride in our meticulous process to ensure the highest quality semi husked coconuts reach our customers worldwide:

    Sourcing from Trusted Farmers

    We acquire semi husked coconuts from trusted farmers in Pollachi for their exceptional taste and quality.

    Quality Control and Sorting

    Upon reaching our facilities, coconuts undergo rigorous quality checks; only top-quality ones proceed for further processing.

    Semi-Husking and Packaging

    We semi-husk coconuts with advanced techniques, carefully package them in eco-friendly materials to preserve freshness and nutrients for export.

    Export Logistics

    We use our network and logistics expertise for timely coconut delivery worldwide, managing shipping by harbour efficiently.

    Quality Assurance

    Our quality assurance team ensures each coconut meets strict standards before shipment, prioritizing customer satisfaction and global delight.
